# Simple YouTube 🎬

Simple YouTube is a lightweight browser extension designed to streamline your YouTube experience by removing unnecessary clutter and providing customizable features.

## Features 🌟

- **Clutter-Free Interface**: Say goodbye to distractions! Simple YouTube can remove clutter such as shorts, "Watch Again" feed, and short videos, allowing you to focus solely on the content you want to watch.

- **Customization**: Tailor your YouTube experience to your preferences. Simple YouTube allows you to configure all settings.

## Installation 💻

### Chrome
1. Download the extension from the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/simple-youtube/iecgbpoggpmlohjeojnjjihabobaaeek).
2. Click "Add to Chrome" to install the extension.
3. Once installed, navigate to YouTube and enjoy the clutter-free experience!

### Firefox
1. Download the extension from [Firefox Add-ons](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/simple-youtube).
2. Click "Add to Firefox" to install the extension.
3. After installation, visit YouTube to start using Simple YouTube.

## Usage 🚀

Once installed, Simple YouTube works silently in the background, automatically removing clutter from YouTube pages. You can customize the extension's settings by accessing the popup, where you can adjust various preferences to suit your needs.
